I.The architecture of a career

Five pillars. One coherent system for the long arc of your career.

Most career tools solve one slice — a CV, a course, a job board, a chat. We tied them all to a single living plan that adapts as you grow and as the market moves.

01

AI-Guided Career Design

A guided conversation with an LLM career coach turns your situation, goals and constraints into a multi-phase, fully editable roadmap.

02

Real-Time Market Intelligence

Live signals from job boards, GitHub, LinkedIn and industry communities — filtered to what actually matters for your plan this week.

03

CV-Based Gap Analysis

Upload a CV and a target role. We parse both, surface the gaps, and convert each one into a concrete project, course or networking action.

04

Plan Execution & Tracking

Weekly time-budget, daily habits with streaks, a Friday review ritual, and quantified progress that turns ambition into measurable execution.

05

Continuous Adaptation

Your plan updates when you finish a milestone, change direction, or when the market shifts. A five-minute chat is enough — no more redrafting.

II.The journey

From CV to career plan, in under fifteen minutes.

A step-by-step onboarding so you always know what's being asked, and what you'll get in return.

01

Upload your CV

Drop in a CV, resume, LinkedIn export, GitHub or portfolio URL. We parse a structured profile of your skills, experience, projects and signals of impact.

02

Set your direction

Pick a target role, paste a job posting, or let the AI suggest careers that fit your strengths, geography, salary range and life constraints.

03

Get your roadmap

The AI generates a personalised, multi-phase plan with milestones, weekly routines, learning resources, projects and networking actions — fully editable.

04

Track & adapt

Daily habits, weekly reviews, market alerts, and live opportunity radar. The roadmap adapts as you complete milestones or as the market shifts.
